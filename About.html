I am a Mine Controller for BHP based in Brisbane's Integrated Remote Operations Centre (IROC), working with fleet management systems to control mine sites based in the Bowen Basin. Before this I was on the other end of the radio as a Haul Truck Operator. In June 2020, I joined the She Codes Plus program, which is a female focused coding program to help more women get into the tech industry. My goal is to use the programming languages taught to help develop user friendly reports and programs to assist in making a Mine Controllers's life in the seat so much easier! Outside of mining, I am an avid indoor plant grower and own the sweetest and most spoilt rescue cat, Pepper.
